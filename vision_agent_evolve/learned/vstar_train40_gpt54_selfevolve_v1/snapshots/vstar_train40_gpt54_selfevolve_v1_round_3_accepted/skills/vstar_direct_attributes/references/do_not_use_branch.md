## Do-Not-Use Branch

This SOP is not appropriate when:
- The question requires counting.
- The question requires reading text.
- The question asks for spatial comparison among multiple similar candidates.
- The answer depends on inference, world knowledge, or multi-step reasoning rather than direct visual inspection.
- The referent is too underspecified to localize reliably.

In these situations, use a different skill family rather than forcing this direct-attribute SOP.
